Provide storage

Give your users remoteStorage accounts

There is an npm package available for building custom remoteStorage servers, called remotestorage-server. It is the basis of the starter-kit and will be updated whenever new versions of the spec come out.

To add remoteStorage support to your online storage product, please check out our forum category and follow our current "live" IETF Internet Draft, which until November 2015 is: draft-dejong-remotestorage-04.

During June - November 2015, all storage providers should switch from version 03 to version 04, and so for each subsequent six-month period (always staying at least 1 and at most 2 versions behind the newest published version).

Versions 2010.12, 2011.04, 2011.12, 2012.04, draft-dejong-remotestorage-00, draft-dejong-remotestorage-01, and draft-dejong-remotestorage-02 are deprecated.
